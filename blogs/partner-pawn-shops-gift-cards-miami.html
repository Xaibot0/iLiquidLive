How to Partner with Miami Pawn Shops to Redeem Gift Cards (and Help Customers Get More)

Looking to collaborate with pawn shops and help your customers convert gift cards into products or cash? Learn how pawn shops profit, what to offer them, and why partnering with Liquid makes perfect sense in Miami.

Most Miami pawn shops overlook a growing customer segment—people willing to trade gift cards for merchandise. But without a fast way to convert those cards into cash, they miss the opportunity entirely. In this blog, we explore how partnering with Liquid enables pawn shops to accept gift cards confidently, boost store traffic, and unlock a new revenue stream.

‍

Pawn shops don’t currently buy gift cards—but they could if they had a streamlined way to process them. Here’s the big opportunity:

  • Customers can pawn gift cards today for merchandise or store credit
  • Pawn shops then redeem those gift cards instantly through Liquid
  • Everyone wins: customers get value, pawn shops add a new revenue channel, and Liquid facilitates payouts

‍

‍

Why This Partnership Works

‍

  1. New revenue stream for pawn shops
    – Pawn shops buy gift cards at a discount (20–60%) and resell to Liquid (80–95%)
  2. Improved customer experience
    – Customers avoid low online offers, pawns gain trust and loyalty
  3. Low effort, high value
    – Liquid handles verification, payout, and fraud protection

‍

What Pawn Shops Need to Start

‍

To make this work, pawn shops should:

  • Accept major gift cards (Amazon, Walmart, Target, Apple)
  • Have a digital screening station to validate the card code
  • Complete a quick balance check via Liquid
  • Partner with Liquid to redeem the card and get paid instantly

‍

Step-by-Step Partnership Process

‍

  1. Liquid provides a simple onboarding session for the shop
  2. The shop places a self-serve gift card kiosk or designated desk
  3. Customers bring in their physical or digital card
  4. Shop staff scans and validates the card in Liquid’s system
  5. Liquid transfers the redemption amount to the shop instantly
  6. Customer walks out with merchandise or shop credit — no fuss

‍

  • Choose a pilot location (e.g., Brickell, Downtown Miami)

  • Launch co-branded marketing: "We accept gift cards" signage
  • Monitor success and replicate across Miami ZIPs

‍

Pawn shops don’t need to wait for gift card demand — they can start with what’s already coming in. Partnering with Liquid makes this fast, simple, and profitable.
